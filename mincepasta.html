How to make a Mince Pasta!

Mince Pasta

Mince Pasta is a very simple and a delicious meal to enjoy for dinner. It will only take 30 minutes to cook. Serve hot with any drink.

Mince Pasta Ingredients

Here are the ingredients that you'll be needing for this recipe.

  • Pasta:

    • 500g of pasta.


  • Salt:

    • A pinch of salt.


  • Tomato:

    • 1 large tomato.


  • Tomato source:

    • Half a cup.


  • Onion:

    • 1 medium sized onions.


  • Carrots

    • 2 medium sized carrots.


  • Chillies

    • 2 chillies.


  • Garlic:

    • Garlic makes it have a strong flavour.


  • Greenpepper, Redpepper and Yellowpepper:

    • 1 of each peppers.


  • Mince Meat:

    • Any mince meat of your liking.


  • Cooking Oil:

    • 3 tablespoons of cooking oil.


  • Any Toppings or spices of your liking
